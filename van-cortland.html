The Places You'll Go!

Van Cortland Park

Cost : Free!
  The 1,146 acres cover ridges and valleys in the northwest section of the Bronx. It's accessible by subway take the 1 train to the very end. It has playing fields of all kinds (you can often catch a game of cricket on the weekend on one of the cricket fields). It has the nation's oldest public golf course that is beautiful so tee up. It has a lake, too. Did I mention the Equestrian Center? Give it an afternoon.
